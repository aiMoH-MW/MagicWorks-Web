## 10. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: How quickly can MagicWorks start?**
A: Discovery call within one working day. Proposal within 5 working days. Onboarding begins after contract sign-off.

**Q: Do you guarantee rankings or leads?**
A: No. MagicWorks guarantees a sound method and transparent attribution, not specific ranking positions or lead volumes. Marketing compounds over time; 12 months is the recommended commitment.

**Q: Can I hire MagicWorks for both strategy and execution?**
A: For Digital Marketing and Web Development, yes — MagicWorks handles both. For AI Consultation and Platform Consultation, MagicWorks is advisory-only. It will advise; you choose who builds.

**Q: Do you work with startups?**
A: MagicWorks works best with businesses ₹10 Cr and above for digital marketing, and ₹25 Cr and above for AI consultation. Very early-stage startups are typically not the right fit.

**Q: What makes MagicWorks different from a typical digital agency?**
A: Three things: (1) AI-native delivery — AI is embedded into websites, campaigns, and strategy, not added as an afterthought. (2) Advisory independence — for AI and platform work, MagicWorks never bundles advice with implementation so recommendations are conflict-free. (3) One team, one invoice — no hand-offs across departments or sub-contractors.

**Q: Do you serve international clients?**
A: Primary focus is India (Pune, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Delhi-NCR). Growing presence in Europe and USA. Discovery call determines fit for international engagements.

**Q: What is AEO?**
A: Answer Engine Optimisation. MagicWorks optimises content to appear in AI-generated answers on platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews — not just traditional search results.

**Q: What is MagicFlow AI?**
A: MagicFlow AI is a sibling SaaS product (separate from MagicWorks services) — a branded AI chatbot that qualifies website visitors and logs leads with campaign context. See https://www.magicflowai.io.

**Q: What is Magic Pipeline?**
A: Magic Pipeline is a sibling SaaS platform — a multi-tenant CRM for agencies and multi-brand teams with email, WhatsApp, SMS, lead scoring, and invoicing. See https://www.magicpipeline.io.

**Q: How do I get started?**
A: Book a 30-minute discovery call at https://magicworksitsolutions.com/contact or email hello@magicworksitsolutions.com. No obligation; MagicWorks will tell you honestly if it is the right fit.
